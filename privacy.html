Presently Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Overview

Presently is designed to help people create intentional presence sessions. The app stores session preferences, progress, and app settings on your device.

Data Stored by the App

Presently stores your local session settings, progress, and app preferences so you can track intentional time. This information is used to provide app features.

Shared Sessions

If shared sessions are enabled, session invitations and participant details may be synced through Apple services such as CloudKit so the shared experience can work across devices.

Purchases

Presently Pro purchases are handled through Apple. Presently uses StoreKit to check purchase status and unlock paid features.

Sale of Personal Information

Presently does not sell personal information.
