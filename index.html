Support

Presently helps people create intentional presence sessions for solo focus, couples, families, meals, study, and shared time.

For support, questions, or feedback, email grtsharon@gmail.com.

Common Questions

  • No account is required to use Presently.
  • Presently Pro is a one-time non-consumable purchase handled through Apple.
  • Anyone can end a session early when needed.
  • Presently is a wellbeing and focus tool. It does not replace parental supervision, professional advice, or personal responsibility.
